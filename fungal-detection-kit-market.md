<p><p>The Fungal Detection Kit market includes various types, such as Antigen or Antibody in a Solid Phase, which involves the immobilization of fungal antigens or antibodies on a solid surface for detection. Enzyme Labeled Antigen or Antibody uses enzymes linked to the antigens or antibodies to facilitate detection through enzyme-substrate reactions. Substrates on Which Enzymes Act serve as the medium where enzymes catalyze reactions, producing measurable signals to identify and quantify fungal presence effectively in clinical or environmental samples.</p></p>
