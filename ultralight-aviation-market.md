<p><p>The ultralight aviation market comprises various segments, including paragliding, paramotors, gyroplanes, and other types. Paramotors combine a backpack-mounted motor with a paraglider, offering powered flight and extended flight capabilities. Gyroplanes, or autogyros, use rotating blades for lift and a propeller for thrust, providing stable and safe flying experiences. Other markets may include ultralight trikes and experimental aircraft, catering to niche flying enthusiasts.</p></p>
