<p><p>The infusion pump software market can be categorized into three types: DERS (Dose Error Reduction Systems), Interoperability, and Clinical Workflow. DERS enhances patient safety by minimizing dosing errors through predefined limits and alerts. Interoperability integrates infusion pumps with electronic health records and other medical devices, enabling seamless data exchange and improving overall care coordination. Clinical Workflow software optimizes the administration process by streamlining workflows, reducing manual tasks, and enabling better tracking of medication administration, ultimately enhancing operational efficiency in healthcare settings.</p></p>
