<p><p>The table tennis rubber market is categorized into various types based on pimple design and characteristics. "Pimples Out" rubbers feature outward-facing pimples, offering maximum spin and control for aggressive play. "Pimples In" rubbers have inward-facing pimples, providing excellent speed and spin while minimizing friction. "Pimples Out (Special)" combines unique pimple shapes for specialized playing styles, enhancing spin and unpredictability. "Long Pimples" are designed for defensive players; they disrupt opponents' rhythm and can produce deceptive spins, making them essential for strategic gameplay.</p></p>
