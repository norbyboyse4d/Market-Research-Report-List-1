<p><ul><li>Glass-Ceramic Powder</li><li>Glass Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LTCC (Low Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) powder market primarily consists of two types: glass-ceramic powder and glass powder. Glass-ceramic powder is a composite material that combines the properties of glass and ceramics, offering enhanced thermal stability and electrical performance, making it suitable for high-frequency applications. On the other hand, glass powder consists of finely milled glass particles that are used to enhance the mechanical and dielectric properties of LTCC substrates, promoting effective co-firing with metal conductors.</p></p>
