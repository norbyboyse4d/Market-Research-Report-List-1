<p><strong>The Knitted Cloth Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Warp Knitting</li><li>Weft Knitting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The knitted cloth market comprises two primary types: warp knitting and weft knitting. Warp knitting involves yarns that run vertically, resulting in strong, stable fabrics ideal for items like activewear and lingerie. In contrast, weft knitting uses horizontal yarns, creating stretchable and softer materials, commonly found in sweaters and T-shirts. Each type caters to different applications and consumer needs, with warp knitting offering durability and weft knitting providing comfort, thereby influencing their respective market dynamics and trends.</p></p>
