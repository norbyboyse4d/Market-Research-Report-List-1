<p><p>One-way clutches with internal bearings are mechanical devices that allow rotation in one direction while preventing it in the opposite direction. The market is segmented into ball bearing clutches and roller bearing clutches. Ball bearing clutches utilize balls to provide low friction and higher speed, making them ideal for applications requiring quick engagement. Roller bearing clutches, on the other hand, use cylindrical rollers, offering greater torque capacity and shock resistance, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types enhance efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
