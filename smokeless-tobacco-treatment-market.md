<p><ul><li>Chewing Tobacco</li><li>Snuff/Dipping Tobacco</li><li>Dissolvable Tobacco</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smokeless tobacco treatment market encompasses three main types: chewing tobacco, snuff/dipping tobacco, and dissolvable tobacco. Chewing tobacco is typically shredded leaves chewed for flavor and nicotine. Snuff or dipping tobacco is a finely ground product placed between the gum and lip, releasing nicotine as saliva mixes with it. Dissolvable tobacco comes in various forms, such as strips or lozenges, which dissolve in the mouth, providing a discreet way to consume nicotine without combustion. Each type poses unique treatment challenges and requires targeted cessation strategies.</p></p>
