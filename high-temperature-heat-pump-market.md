<p><p>The high temperature heat pump market includes several types designed for efficient heating. Air to water monobloc heat pumps integrate all components in a single unit for easy installation. Air to water cylinder heat pumps connect to an external tank, enhancing storage capabilities. Groundwater heat pumps utilize subterranean water to generate heat efficiently. Split air-to-water heat pumps divide the system into two units, optimizing space and efficiency. Exhaust air heat pumps recover heat from indoor air, increasing energy efficiency in buildings.</p></p>
