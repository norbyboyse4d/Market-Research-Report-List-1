<p><p>The pallet handling equipment market comprises two primary types: automatic and manual systems. Automatic pallet handling equipment employs advanced technology to streamline processes, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor costs. This includes automated guided vehicles and robotic systems that can move pallets without human intervention. In contrast, manual pallet handling equipment relies on human operators for tasks such as lifting, stacking, and transporting pallets, often utilizing tools like pallet jacks or forklifts. Both types serve essential roles in warehouse and logistics operations.</p></p>
