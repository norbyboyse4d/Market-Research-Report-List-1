<p><strong>The Optical Bypass Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Bypass Relay market comprises two primary types: Single Mode and Multimode. Single Mode fibers utilize a single light path, enabling long-distance communication with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and high-speed networks. In contrast, Multimode fibers allow multiple light paths, facilitating shorter-distance data transmission and often used in local area networks. The choice between these types depends on distance, bandwidth requirements, and application needs, influencing network design and overall efficiency in data transmission.</p></p>
