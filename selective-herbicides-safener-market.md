<p><ul><li>Pre-emergence</li><li>Post-emergence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Selective Herbicides Safener Market is divided into two main applications: pre-emergence and post-emergence. Pre-emergence safeners are applied before weed germination, protecting crops from potential herbicide damage while preventing weed growth. In contrast, post-emergence safeners are utilized after crops and weeds have emerged, helping to mitigate herbicide injury to the crops while controlling existing weeds. Both applications enhance crop safety and effectiveness of herbicide treatments, promoting sustainable agriculture practices while maintaining crop yields.</p></p>
