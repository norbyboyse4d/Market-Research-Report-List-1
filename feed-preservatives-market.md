<p><p>The feed preservatives market comprises various types. Feed acidifiers enhance digestion and lower pH in animal feed, promoting gut health. Mold inhibitors prevent fungal growth, preserving feed quality and safety. Feed antioxidants protect nutrients from oxidation, extending shelf life and maintaining nutritional value. Anticaking agents ensure free-flowing feed by preventing clumping, improving consistency during handling and feeding. Collectively, these preservatives enhance animal health, improve feed efficiency, and contribute to overall livestock productivity.</p></p>
