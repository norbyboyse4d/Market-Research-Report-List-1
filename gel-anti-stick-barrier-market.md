<p><p>The Gel Anti-stick Barrier market is divided into two primary types: absorbable and non-absorbable barriers. Absorbable barriers are designed to be gradually absorbed by the body over time, reducing the risk of adhesions while promoting natural healing. In contrast, non-absorbable barriers remain in the body, providing long-term protection against tissue adhesion.
