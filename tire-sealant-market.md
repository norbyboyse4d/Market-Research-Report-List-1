<p><p>The tire sealant market is primarily divided into two types: latex-based sealants and latex-free sealants. Latex-based sealants use natural or synthetic latex to create a flexible and effective barrier against punctures, making them widely popular among cyclists and motorists. In contrast, latex-free sealants utilize alternative materials, such as polymers or special compounds, catering to users with sensitivities to latex or those seeking eco-friendly options. Both types serve to enhance tire performance and extend lifespan by preventing air loss from punctures.</p></p>
