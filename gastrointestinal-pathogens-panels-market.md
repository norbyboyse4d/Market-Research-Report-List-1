Bacterial panels target pathogenic bacteria such as Salmonella, E. coli, and Campylobacter, responsible for foodborne illnesses. Parasite panels identify parasites like Giardia and Cryptosporidium, causing gastrointestinal infections. Together, these panels enhance diagnostic accuracy, facilitate timely treatment, and improve patient outcomes by enabling healthcare providers to identify the specific pathogens causing gastrointestinal diseases.</p></p>
