<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Dual Sensors</li><li>Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart fire alarm market encompasses three main types: photoelectric, dual sensors, and ionization alarms. Photoelectric alarms detect smoldering fires by sensing smoke particles, making them effective for early detection. Ionization alarms excel at identifying fast-flaming fires using radioactive material to sense changes in ionization levels. Dual sensor alarms combine both technologies, offering comprehensive coverage for various fire types. Each type provides unique advantages, catering to different residential and commercial safety needs, ultimately enhancing fire prevention and response strategies.</p></p>
