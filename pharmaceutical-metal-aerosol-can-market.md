<p><p>The pharmaceutical metal aerosol can market primarily consists of two types: aluminum and tinplate. Aluminum cans are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and offer excellent barrier protection, making them ideal for products requiring long shelf life and airtight storage. Tinplate cans, made from steel coated with tin, provide durability and a classic appearance, often preferred for specific formulations. Both types are commonly used in packaging pharmaceutical products such as inhalers, sprays, and topical solutions, catering to diverse consumer needs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
