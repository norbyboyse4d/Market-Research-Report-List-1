<p><p>The biopreservation market comprises two main types: home-brew media and pre-formulated media. Home-brew media are customized formulations created in-house by laboratories or facilities, offering flexibility and specific nutrient adjustments for different microbial requirements. In contrast, pre-formulated media are commercially prepared solutions designed for specific applications, ensuring consistency and reliability in results. Both types serve essential roles in extending the shelf life of products by utilizing natural preservatives to inhibit microbial growth and maintain quality during storage.</p></p>
