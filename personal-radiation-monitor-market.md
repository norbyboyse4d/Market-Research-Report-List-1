<p><strong>The Personal Radiation Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Type</li><li>Passive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Personal Radiation Monitor market consists of two primary types: active and passive monitors. Active monitors continuously measure and display radiation levels in real-time, providing immediate feedback to users, which is crucial for safety in high-radiation environments. Conversely, passive monitors, such as film badges or dosimeters, accumulate radiation exposure over time and require analysis after the exposure period to determine doses received. Each type serves unique needs, making them essential tools for personal safety in various industries exposed to radiation.</p></p>
